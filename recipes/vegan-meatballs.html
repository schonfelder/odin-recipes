Description

These vegan meatballsa are the perfect substitute for regular meatballs. Will make everyone jealous of your cooking skills.

Ingredients

For Meatballs

  1. 80 g white onion (minced)
  2. 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  3. 7 g flaxseed meal (to make flax egg)
  4. 37 ml water (to make flax egg)
  5. 227 g tempeh (or as recipe is written, sub 1 heaping cup rinsed and drained chickpeas for similar result)
  6. 27 g vegan parmesan cheese
  7. 2 tsp Italian seasonings (or 1/2 tsp each dried basil and oregano)
  8. 8 g fresh parsley (optional)
  9. 34 g vegan bread crumbs (gluten-free for GF eaters // or sub almond meal)
  10. 30 ml your favorite marinara or tomato sauce
  11. Olive oil (for saut√©ing)
  12. Salt & pepper (to taste)

For Coating

  1. 23 g vegan bread crumbs (gluten-free for GF eaters)
  2. 27 g vegan parmesan cheese
